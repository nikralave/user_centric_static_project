My Story

I started working in the Travel Industry 15 years ago after returning from backpacking around South East Asia, combining my love for travel with a career always seemed the obvious choice for me.

As I began to develop my career, a passion to strive for the highest levels of customer service also came to the forefront. Hearing that a customer loved my recommendations for their holiday and wanted to travel again is one of the reasons I find this job so rewarding.

I have many travel highlights, of which honeymooning in the Maldives is definitely up there, it really is hard to top the sun, sea and sand there but equally memorable is spotting lions & leopards in Kruger, hitting the open road in the US, climbing Sigiriya rock in Sri Lanka or experimenting with street food in Bangkok to name but a few.

Working with Holidaysplease allows me to be 100% independent, to ensure I can supply you with the right holiday with the most appropriate tour operator. Over the years I have gained the experience to find you the perfect fly and flop holiday or tailor a multicentre adventure. Attention to detail is as important to me as it is to you, I am afforded the time to spend ensuring we craft the perfect holiday, I am available evenings and weekends allowing me to fit with your schedule.

As a typical 'travel geek' I am always keeping up to date on the newest hotel opening or hottest destination this year, there is a very big world out there to explore and it would be my pleasure to help you find the right destination to discover next.
